500+ awesome bookmarks for Data Science & Machine Learning

This is a list of free online data science & machine learning resources that I built over the last year. There’s a wealth of information on the web, and as a data science professional, I would often lose the really good stuff in the ocean of data science resources. That’s when I started organizing everything in one place — the bookmarks toolbar — easy to access, share and maintain.

This list consists of articles, tutorials, blogs, and other interesting stuff related to data science — things that I find useful, and you may too.

How to use this list

When you are trying to understand/use PCA, but it fails to make sense, that’s when you go to the ‘PCA’ section and read a couple of articles about the intuition behind PCA.

If you just want to read something cool about what’s happening in the world of AI, head to the ‘Articles’ section and you’ll find something interesting.

You get the idea..

Note: You can directly right-click and open any link on this page, or download the HTML file from here and import this list as bookmarks in your favorite browser!

Is there a better way to organize?

Of course! And I do that everyday. As I discover new content, the folders and sub-folders in my bookmarks toolbar change and evolve. Make sure you create a system that works for you.
